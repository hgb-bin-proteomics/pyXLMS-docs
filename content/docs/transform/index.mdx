# Introduction

## Available Data Transformations

pyXLMS currently features several different functions to summarize, validate, and transform your data:

- [Result summaries, dataframes, and utility functions](/docs/transform/summary_and_util)
- [Uniqueness and aggregation](/docs/transform/aggregate)
- [False-Discovery-Rate estimation and validation](/docs/transform/validate)
- [Annotating estimated False-Discovery-Rate](/docs/transform/annotate_fdr)
- [Filtering](/docs/transform/filter)
- [Annotation of protein accessions and protein crosslink positions](/docs/transform/reannotate_positions)
- [Intersections of results](/docs/transform/intersection)
